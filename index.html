About Us

Ameen Jali House is a well-known place for high-quality wire mesh and jali products. We make different types of jalis that are used for fencing, construction, animal cages, and ventilation. Our jalis are strong, long-lasting, and used all over Pakistan.

We provide the best wire mesh solutions for goats, sheep, poultry cages, bird enclosures, and general building use. All our jalis are made with care and are available in different materials and styles to match your needs.

Chikni Jali – Smooth and strong stainless steel mesh
PVC Coated Jali – Weatherproof and colorful mesh
SS Crimped Wire Mesh – Strong grip and heavy-duty use
SS Steel Mesh – Rust-free and long-lasting
SS Wire Mesh – Best for fencing and building support
Welded Wire Mesh – Good for security and factory use
Used in goat sheds, poultry farms, and animal cages
Also perfect for fencing, air ventilation, and safety covers
